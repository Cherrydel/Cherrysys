CHERRY_DEL TERMINAL system administration software for embedded systems.

The system includes

OS_DEL: The operating unit of the system. This module has a system for modules and the development of your models, as well as access to the Server in the system.DELL server
management can be performed directly from your hardware. The special MY_SQL_DEL module allows you to use our databases. Soon we will allow you to create your databases and connect them to our system.

GAMES_DEL: The game complex is designed for remote connection to popular games, for playing with friends. In the upcoming update, we will allow you to create your servers in a few clicks of the GAME: GTA-V
